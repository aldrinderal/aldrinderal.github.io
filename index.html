About

I am Aldrin Deral A, a portrait artist from Tenkasi, Tamil Nadu, currently pursuing a degree in Engineering.

My passion for art began during my school years and gradually evolved from a hobby into a dedicated artistic pursuit, with portrait drawing becoming my primary focus.

I have participated in numerous art competitions and achieved recognition at higher levels, including selection at the State Level Drawing Competition conducted at Anna University, Chennai.

My work centers on realistic portrait art, where I aim to capture emotion, personality, and fine detail in every piece. Each portrait is carefully hand-drawn to transform meaningful moments into timeless artworks.
